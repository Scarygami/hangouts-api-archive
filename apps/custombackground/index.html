Custom Background

Description

This Hangout app enables you to easily use photos you have uploaded to Google+ as a background replacement for video calls.


Usage/Installation

You can simply start using the app in a Google+™ Hangout by clicking on the button below.

Please make sure to read and agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy before using the app.



If this is your first time using the app, you will be asked to authorize it. Afterwards you can use the "Select/upload photo" button to select one of your Google+ Photos or upload a new one.



You can then either keep the "Auto Adjustment" option enabled, or disable it and use the slider to control how much of the video should be replaced with your selected background.
